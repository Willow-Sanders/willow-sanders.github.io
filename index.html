Real-Time Reservoir and River Monitoring Platform
Created a web app that ingested public data on reservoir and river heights to inform the Trinity River Authority on when to release water from reservoirs. Actively used by Trinity River Employee managers in daily operations.
Tools used: Microservice Architecture, Event-Driven Architecure, Kubernetes Docker, Python, Flask Digital Ocean, RabbitMQ
